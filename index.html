About Me

Artist, Designer, and Programmer!

I am a Visualization major and Computer Science minor at Texas A&M University. I enjoy designing and devloping video games, character design, UI/UX design, and understanding the psychology of games. I am currently enthralled with the psychology of horror games and what makes consumers fascinated with the feeling of fear. I grew up with a passion for technology, and learned to love the intersection of art and computing.
